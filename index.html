our education

Coding and Robotics

This lesson introduces children to the world of programming and robotics. Students will learn the basics of coding using kid-friendly programming languages as well as assemble and program a simple robot. The goal is to develop critical thinking skills, logic and technical creativity.

Language Classes

Language classes offer foreign language learning such as English, Mandarin or Spanish. The program includes teaching basic vocabulary, grammar, speaking, listening, reading and writing. The lessons are designed to expand children's communication skills and open their minds to other cultures.

Drawing Lessons

Drawing lessons give children the opportunity to express their creativity through visual arts. Students will learn various drawing techniques, use of colors, as well as an introduction to various art mediums such as pencils, watercolors, and crayons. This lesson also teaches about the basic elements of art such as line, shape, and texture.
